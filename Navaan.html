Navaan Sandhu

About :

Navaan Sandhu is an Indian singer who has achieved recognition for his work in the music industry. His trademark style of R&B and hip-hop has seen him earn respect and admiration throughout India and beyond. Sandhu is a highly versatile artist who delivers popular and unique renditions of traditional Punjabi, Bollywood, hip-hop, and rap music. He has collaborated with many of the biggest names in the music industry, such as Guardian Pingle, DJ Wale Badshah, and BSF. Navaan Sandhu is a popular Indian singer and lyricist known for his unique fusion of Punjabi and urban music. He has become one of the most sought-after independent singers in India. With the release of his debut album 'Mashup - The Walls, Navaan has established himself as an artist to be reckoned with.
